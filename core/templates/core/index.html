About Runners Hive

Hi, we are Kathrin and Sarah!

We created Runners Hive to connect passionate runners with the vibrant running community in Berlin. The idea was inspired by the recent growth of run clubs and running events in the city. While the running scene has become increasingly vibrant, it is also fragmented across different websites, social media groups, and platforms, making it difficult for both organizers and runners to stay informed. We built this platform to provide a unified space where organizers can promote their events, and runners can easily discover and join them.
